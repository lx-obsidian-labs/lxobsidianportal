About Us

Building Mining Professionals Since 1998

Middelburg Mining Institute has been at the forefront of mining education for over two decades. Located in the heart of South Africa's mining region, we provide hands-on training that prepares students for real-world challenges.

Our state-of-the-art facilities and experienced instructors ensure that graduates are ready to contribute from day one in the mining industry.
